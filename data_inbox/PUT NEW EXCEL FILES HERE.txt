Drop a new full-data Excel workbook (.xlsx, with a "DATA" sheet in the usual
format) into THIS folder.

If automatic updates are turned on (RGT Dashboard.bat -> "Set up automatic
updates"), the dashboard will rebuild itself within a minute or two and the new
numbers appear after the shared server restarts. The processed file is moved into
the "processed" subfolder with a timestamp, so you always know what was loaded.

K-S sites (Lodgepole, Plan D) are kept automatically - they are not in the INW
workbook, so they are preserved from the previous data.
